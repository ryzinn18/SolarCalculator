How To Use The Solar Calculator

Using The Solar Calculator is simple! All you need is your energy consumption & cost over the past year. You can acquire this easily from your utility company online or over the phone. Once you have this, enter your consumption info and address into the section below and hit the Run Tool button.

Note: that you do not need to use your personal address. You can use a nearby one, such as your local library. Or, you could just enter your city and state. Keep in mind, the closer the address you enter is to your actual residence, the more accurate your results are!

Note: energy consumption must be entered in kiloWattHours (kWh) per month. Your utility company will likely provide your consumption in this metric. If not, Google offers simple conversion tools.
